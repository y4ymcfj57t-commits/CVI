# Phase I design patterns (quick library)

- **Single target rule:** 1 item/idea visible at once
- **Plain background:** solid matte surface, minimal contrast noise
- **High salience, low clutter:** bold simple shape; avoid textured fills
- **Latency script:** present → pause → wait → then speak
- **Micro-sessions:** 1–3 minutes on, then break
- **Confirm by touch/audio:** built-in, not “extra”
